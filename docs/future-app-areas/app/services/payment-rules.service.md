# Payment Rules Service

Future responsibility: manage configuration for hiding payment methods.

## Responsibilities

- Manage payment method mappings.
- Manage payment hide rules.
- Support conditions based on selected shipping method, pincode group, product tags, and cart/order conditions.
- Write published configuration for the payment customization Function.

## Constraint

Payment method names must be admin-configured mappings. They must not be hardcoded in checkout logic.
