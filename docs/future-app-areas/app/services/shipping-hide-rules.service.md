# Shipping Hide Rules Service

Future responsibility: manage configuration for hiding shipping methods.

## Responsibilities

- Manage enabled and disabled hide rules.
- Reference admin-managed shipping method mappings.
- Support pincode, area group, product tag, cutoff, and cart/customer conditions as needed.
- Write published rule configuration for the delivery customization Function.

## Constraint

Shipping method names must be configured through admin mappings or imported configuration. They must not be embedded in app logic.
